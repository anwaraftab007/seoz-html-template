Our Values

Our values and beliefs are at the heart of everything we do and create.

Everyone has different methods and capacities for learning, thus requiring learning methods tailored to their unique needs to ensure they succeed. Our ethos was to build adaptive and flexible learning tools to enable everyone to learn at their pace.

Learning is hard, especially when it comes to complex and nuanced subjects, with realworld application forming a critical part of the educational journey. So we set out to make learning fun and engaging, but also applicable in the real world.

Less than 27% of young adults and 5% of teens have access to structured financial education. We created CredAhead to provide universal access to Free financial education - no hidden fees. This line is hidden but takes up space

We believe that financial education should begin as early as 13, but its never too late to learn and optimise financial health. Our platform can be used by anyone, irrespective of their age background or circumstance

Our Approach

CredAhead brings together the best practices from across multiple spheres to redefine Financial Education & Inclusion

Subjects are broken down into smaller modules with 10-minute quizzes creating easily digestible content, with scheduled repetition of concepts to ensure memory retention.

We leverage AI to craft highly personalised learning experiences, with our unique algorithm creating a unique learning path based on specific user's needs.

Our Knowledge Graph forms the baseline for the learning module which organisesthe complex nuances of our learning modules to ensure a rounded education.

Our algorithm adjusts the difficulty level based on user's responses, with secure data collection and analysis for accurate psychometric calibration.

Our robust gamification layer provides users with rewards and levels which are tied to lesson completion, mastering concepts and demonstrating positive financial habits.
